About Us

We are team Tech Next

With the vision to make our country clean and green, we are working on this project called Go-Kachra, which aims at manufacturing fuel and fertilizer from food wastes produced at our houses and agricultural wastes at farmlands, we can collectively work for well-being of our nation. Fuel thus produced will be used to blend petrol. Blended petrol will be cheaper and will be available to all at low prices, thus reducing daily expenses on running on auto-mobiles also it will help us to reduce dependency on OPEC countries for fuel requirement along with it, we will save huge amount of foreign money. On the other end we are keeping our country clean.

We invite all Indians to join this movement Go-Kachra and support us by donating us food wastes.
